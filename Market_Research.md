# Market Research

## Executive Summary

Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) contribute significantly to the economy but often struggle to access quick business financing. Traditional loan approval processes are slow, paper-intensive, and require multiple branch visits. This project proposes an AI-powered digital lending platform to improve speed, transparency, and customer experience.

---

# Industry Overview

Digital lending is one of the fastest-growing segments in banking. Banks are increasingly adopting AI, automation, OCR, and digital onboarding to reduce operational costs and accelerate loan approvals.
