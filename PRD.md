# Product Requirement Document (PRD)

## Product Name
AI-Powered SME Loan Platform

## Objective
Develop an AI-powered platform that enables Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) to apply for business loans digitally, receive instant eligibility results, and track loan applications in real time.

## Problem Statement
Current SME loan approval processes are slow, paper-based, and require multiple branch visits. This leads to poor customer experience and higher operational costs for banks.

## Target Users

### Primary Users
- Small Business Owners
- Startup Founders
- MSMEs

### Secondary Users
- Relationship Managers
- Credit Officers
- Operations Team

## User Goals
- Apply for loans online
- Know eligibility instantly
- Upload documents securely
- Track application status
- Receive quick approval

## Functional Requirements

### User Registration
- Email Login
- Mobile Login
- OTP Verification

### Loan Eligibility
- AI Eligibility Checker
- Business Details Form
- Credit Score Check

### Document Upload
- Aadhaar
- PAN
- GST Certificate
- Bank Statements

### Dashboard
- Loan Status
- EMI Details
- Notifications

## Success Metrics
- Loan Approval Time
- Customer Satisfaction (CSAT)
- Loan Conversion Rate
- Monthly Active Users (MAU)

## Future Enhancements
- AI Chatbot
- Voice Assistant
- OCR Document Reading
- Fraud Detection
- Personalized Loan Offers
